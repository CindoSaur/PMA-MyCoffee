Our Story

Born from a love of real food and a desire to make healthy living delicious and accessible.

Farm to your door since 2021

PolyCoffee was founded by two friends frustrated with juice bars using concentrates, additives and sugar-loaded fruit. We wanted something honest.

We partner exclusively with organic farms within 50km of Ho Chi Minh City. Every morning, we cold-press fresh produce and bottle it for same-day delivery — no pasteurisation, no preservatives, no shortcuts.

Our mission is simple: make the healthiest drink you'll have all day also the best-tasting one.
